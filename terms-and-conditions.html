TERMS AND CONDITIONS

AMBASSADORS Limousine Service

1. Billing

We are committed to transparency in our billing process to ensure you understand the charges associated with your reservation.

Reservation Confirmation:

  • A confirmation email will be sent upon booking, detailing the trip type, duration, and estimated costs.
  • Any applicable deposits will also be outlined in the confirmation.

Billing Structure:

Base Rate: Includes the agreed-upon trip duration and any services included in your package.

Additional Charges: May include:

  • Overage and wait time fees
  • Tolls, parking fees, or other third-party expenses
  • Additional stops or changes to the itinerary

Gratuity: A standard gratuity of 20% is added unless otherwise stated.

Payment Methods:

  • Accepted payment methods include credit cards, PayPal, and cash.
  • A valid payment method is required at the time of booking.

Post-Trip Invoice:

  • After the trip, a detailed invoice will be emailed within 24 hours.
  • The invoice will itemize all charges.

Payment for any additional charges is due upon receipt of the invoice.

2. Trip Options

Point-to-Point Transfers:

  • Includes direct transportation between two locations
  • Fixed price with no additional time included beyond the trip duration

Hourly Rentals:

  • Flexible transportation charged by the hour
  • Minimum rental period of 3 hours

Special Event Packages:

  • Tailored for weddings, proms, and other events
  • Package pricing includes specific time blocks and services

Airport Transfers:

  • Flat-rate service for transportation to and from the airport
  • Includes 50 minutes for delays or baggage handling

3. Car Seats

To ensure the safety of all passengers, especially young children, we adhere to strict car seat guidelines.

Car Seat Requirements:

  • Infants (Under 2 Years Old): Rear-facing car seat required
  • Toddlers (2 to 4 Years Old): Forward-facing car seat with harness required
  • Young Children (4 to 8 Years Old): Booster seat required until height of 4'9" or age 8

Client Responsibility:

  • Clients MUST inform us at booking if a car seat is required
  • Clients may provide their own car seats
  • We provide car seats upon request for additional fee

Note: Passengers not adhering to car seat policy may not be permitted to travel.

4. Cleaning

We take pride in providing a clean and comfortable environment for all passengers.

Cleaning Fee Applies For:

  • Spills of food or beverages
  • Bodily fluids or biohazard cleanup
  • Excessive trash or debris
  • Stains from glitter, confetti, or makeup

Fee Amount:

The cleaning fee starts at $300 and may increase depending on required cleaning.

Process:

  • Chauffeur will document vehicle condition with photos
  • Client will be notified immediately if fee applies
  • Detailed report with photos included in invoice

5. Damages

Repair charges apply for any damage caused to the vehicle during reservation.

Examples Include:

  • Tears, burns, or stains on upholstery
  • Damage to electronic systems
  • Broken glass or fixtures
  • Graffiti or markings

Process:

  • Chauffeur will document damages with photos and report
  • Client will be informed immediately
  • Professional assessment of repair costs

Payment:

  • Repair charges due within 7 days of invoice
  • Pre-authorized payments will be processed automatically

Important: Severe damages may result in additional fees for loss of service revenue.

5. Drugs & Alcohol

At AMBASSADORS, the safety and comfort of all passengers and chauffeurs are our highest priority.

Permitted Alcohol Use:

Alcoholic Beverages:

  • Passengers 21+ may consume alcohol responsibly
  • All beverages must remain within the vehicle

Prohibited Behavior:

  • Excessive consumption leading to disruptive behavior
  • Open containers outside the vehicle

Prohibited Drug Use:

  • Use, possession, or distribution of illegal substances is forbidden
  • Cannabis consumption inside the vehicle is prohibited

Enforcement:

  • Chauffeurs may terminate trips for safety violations
  • Incidents will be documented with photos/reports

Consequences:

Violation Action
Trip termination No refund for unused service
Illegal activities Reported to authorities
Repeat offenses Banned from future services
