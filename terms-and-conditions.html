3. Car Seats

To ensure the safety of all passengers, especially young children, we adhere to strict car seat guidelines:

Car Seat Requirements:

  • Infants (Under 2 Years Old): A rear-facing car seat is required.
  • Toddlers (2 to 4 Years Old): A forward-facing car seat with a harness is required.
  • Young Children (4 to 8 Years Old): A booster seat is required until the child reaches a height of 4 feet 9 inches or the age of 8, whichever comes first.

Installation:

  • Clients are encouraged to verify the installation of their car seats before the trip begins.

Exceptions: Passengers not adhering to the car seat policy MAY NOT be permitted to travel, as safety regulations must be strictly followed.
