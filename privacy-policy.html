PRIVACY POLICY

AMBASSADORS Limousine Service Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/01/2025

At AMBASSADORS Limousine Service ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (www.ambassadors.limo) or any of our services, including reservations, city tours, airport transfers, and special event transportation.

1. What We Collect

a. Personal Information (collected during booking or inquiries)

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment details (processed via secure third-party platforms)
  • Pickup/drop-off addresses
  • Date/time of service
  • Flight details (for airport transfers)
  • Tour preferences or special requests

b. Automated Data (collected via website)

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address
  • Location (approximate, for service relevance)
  • Pages visited and interaction time

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and confirm bookings made via our reservation platform
  • Send booking confirmations and driver status updates
  • Coordinate logistics for services including city tours, corporate travel, and special events
  • Send marketing or promotional offers (only if you opt in)
  • Improve website functionality and customer experience

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted partners like our booking platform (e.g., Moovs) and payment processors
  • Drivers or affiliates fulfilling your service request
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use:

  • SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data
  • Secure third-party systems for payments and customer data

5. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we have on file
  • Ask for corrections or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time

Email us at info@ambassadors.limo to make any requests.

6. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance browsing experience
  • Track booking conversions
  • Analyze traffic using tools like Google Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party platforms (e.g., Moovs, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
